About the Ick Factor Test

How the Ick Factor Test Works

The Ick Factor Test presents 20 common behaviors across categories like dating, hygiene, social media, and communication. Swipe right if the behavior gives you the ick, left if it is fine. Your total count and category breakdown reveal your ick sensitivity profile.

Understanding Your Ick Sensitivity

An ick is that sudden feeling of cringe or repulsion toward a behavior. Everyone has different ick triggers. This test helps you discover yours in a fun way and compare with friends to see who is the pickiest!
